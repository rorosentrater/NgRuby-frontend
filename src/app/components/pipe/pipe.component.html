Without pipe:

{{myText}}

With pipe:

{{myText | butts}}

There are tons of good builtins. Look before making your own. Most can also be configured with params. Here is the same number displayed with the currency pipe using different params

  • {{myNumber | currency}}
  • {{myNumber | currency : 'USD' : 'symbol' : '1.0-0'}}
  • {{myNumber | currency:'JPY'}}
